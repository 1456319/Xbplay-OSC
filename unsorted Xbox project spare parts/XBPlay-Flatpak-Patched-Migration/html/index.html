General Tips

  • Press the Guide button by holding the view button (top left) and the menu button (top right) at the same time.
  • Quit a game with the side menu or by stopping the stream within the Xbox. For example, Guide->Stop Remote Play.
  • Use F11 or F10 to toggle fullscreen and use the top left corner to drag the window.
  • Change the streaming resolution and bitrate in the in-game "Options Side Menu->Settings->Advanced".
  • Hide the touch controls in the in-game "Options Side Menu->Settings->Display->Touch Control Behavior".

xCloud Shortcuts [Steam App Required]

  • Add an xCloud shortcuts directly to Steam by clicking on an xCloud game and pressing 'Add to Steam'
  • If you don't see the 'Add to Steam' option, ensure the Steam app is running. The status of the Steam app is displayed on the bottom right of the main menu.
  • When an xCloud shortcut is added to Steam, it will appear in your 'Non Steam' game library after you restart your Steam Deck or the Steam app.
  • Artwork will automatically be added to your xCloud Steam shortcut.
  • If your xCloud artwork gets out of sync, press the sync button in the settings to redownload and override all xCloud title artwork.

Remote Play Setup

  • If you see a yellow popup after login, you do not have remote features enabled. To enable it, signed into your profile on your Xbox and go to 'Settings > Devices & connections > Remote features' and ensure its checked. If it is already checked, toggle it off/on and click 'Test Remote Play'.
  • If you have an IPv6 or DualStack router, remote play should work from outside of your home network. If you have an IPv4 only router, you must enable port forwarding for port 9002 for both UDP and TCP on your router for your consoles IP address.

Command Line Args

  • --xhome Add the xhome launch param to automatically login and start an xhome stream for your default console.
  • --xcloud=titleId Add the xcloud launch param to automatically login and start an xcloud stream on start. The titleId should be the titleId of the game you want to play. A full list of all titleIds can be found online.
